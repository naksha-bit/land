Why BCA

BACHELOR OF COMPUTER APPLICATION.

Bachelor in Computer Application course is designed to develop knowledge of computer and its applications. This program aims to focus on the core fundamentals of computer science and related applications. Emphasis are given on use of latest technologies to work in various sectors of Computer Applications like internet technologies, E-business environment etc. With the growing demand for professionals in IT sector, BCA course is new age degree course in the field of computer applications. Students who opt for BCA are well-equipped to work as professionals in the fields of software development, design and implementation.!

Admission Procedure

Interested Candidates can obtain Application forms from Admission Office at IMS, Ghaziabad.Candidates can also fill the Online inquiry form by clicking on the following link:- http://www.imsuc.ac.in/apply-online-query.php
Registration Process starts from January 2019.
Candidates seeking admission in BCA are required to meet the Admission Counselor in the Institute Premises.
Admission is confirmed on the basis of merit & interaction.
Candidate having 1 or 2 Years gap after XII need to submit a Gap Year Certificate.



Eligibility Criteria


Candidates seeking admission to the BCA Programme must have passed 10+2 (or its equivalent) examination from a recognized Board/ University.


Or


Candidates appearing for +2 examination or awaiting their results are also eligible to apply. Their candidature, however, shall be considered, subject to their clearing the qualifying examination.


Selection Procedure:


10+2 in any stream (Mathematics in High School) with a minimum of 45% marks for General and OBC candidates and pass marks for SC and ST candidates.

How to Apply


Apply online form the website www.imsuc.ac.in. Interested candidates can obtain application forms from admission office at IMS, Ghaziabad. Candidates can also fill the online inquiry form by clicking on the following link:- http://www.imsuc.ac.in/apply-online-query.php.


Registration Process starts from January 2019


Candidates seeking admission in BCA are required to meet the Admission Counselor in the Institute Premises


Admission is confirmed on the basis of merit & interaction


Candidate having 1 or 2 Years gap after XII need to submit a Gap Year Certificate.

Documents Required


Photocopy of X, XII and Graduation mark sheets (With Originals).

Photocopy of Passing Certificate of Graduation (With Originals).

Photocopy of Migration Certificate (With Originals).

4 recent Passport size photographs.

Gap Year Certificate (In case Gap of 1 or 2 years after Graduation).
